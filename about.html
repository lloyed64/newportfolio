Designing to solve problems. I’m Lloyed Cho and I’m passionate about creating user experiences and interfaces that find simple solutions to complex issues.

As far as I know, during my childhood, I always had an affinity for drawing. As a child I never imagined myself becoming an artist when I grew up, but always admired great visual works of art and wanted to imitate it to the best of my abilities. At the start of college, I didn’t think about art as a long term career so I didn’t pursue it. However, as I started getting older my interest shifted so I swapped majors and decided to pursue art at the University of Arizona College of Fine Arts where I received my BFA in graphic design


After graduating I wanted to learn more about UX/UI design because I always been so curious about how good design can influence and direct people. I also found design to be important because it is very personable and it allows the designers to create something elegant and functional for the people. In 2019 , I completed a nine-month mentorship focused on UI/UX design and Frontend Development. This program integrates on all aspects of UI/UX design and the foundation of Frontend Development.
